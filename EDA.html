* The scatterplot (Age vs Fare) indicated no clear linear relationship between age and fare, although higher fares are generally associated with lower passenger class.

* The pairplot confirmed that higher fares and lower passenger class (1st and 2nd) are associated with higher survival rates.

* The heatmap showed that Fare is positively correlated with Survived, while Pclass is negatively correlated with Survived.
Age has only weak correlations with the other variables.

B] Overall Insight:
Passengers who paid higher fares and were in higher classes (1st/2nd class) had a much higher chance of survival, 
while age alone did not significantly influence the outcome.
